About this Apartment

We are pleased to offer temporary residential accommodations for individuals requiring housing for one month or more. Whether you are in the process of relocation, undergoing home renovations, on a professional assignment, or working in a medical or travel-based capacity, we welcome all qualified applicants seeking mid-term housing solutions.

Currently available is a newly renovated second-floor apartment offering approximately 691 square feet of modern, comfortable living space.
Key features include:

– Pet-friendly policy
– In-unit laundry
– Complimentary off-street parking
– Video tour available upon request prior to booking.
– All utilities included in the rent, including high-speed internet.

The Space

Included Amenities

  • Designated off-street parking for one vehicle.
  • High-speed Verizon Fios Gigabit internet for seamless work-from-home or streaming capabilities.
  • In-unit washer and dryer (2-in-1 combo unit) capable of completing a full laundry cycle in approximately two hours.
  • 55-inch Smart TV, compatible with all major streaming platforms and subscription services.
  • Fully equipped kitchen with essential cookware, dinnerware, and utensils.
  • Coffee options include a Keurig k-cup machine (complimentary pods and sweeteners provided) and a standard drip coffee setup.
  • Blackout curtains and white noise machine provided to accommodate varied sleep schedules.
  • Dedicated workstation with ergonomic chair to support remote work and study.
  • Alexa smart device to assist with music playback, weather updates, and general voice-activated information needs.

Commute & Accessibility
