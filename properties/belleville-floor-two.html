About this Apartment

We are pleased to offer temporary residential accommodations for individuals requiring housing for one month or more. Whether you are in the process of relocation, undergoing home renovations, on a professional assignment, or working in a medical or travel-based capacity, we welcome all qualified applicants seeking mid-term housing solutions.

Currently available is a newly renovated second-floor apartment offering approximately 691 square feet of modern, comfortable living space.
Key features include:

– Pet-friendly policy
– In-unit laundry
–Complimentary off-street parking
– Video tour available upon request prior to booking.
